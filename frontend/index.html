ABOUT

The Hidden Gems mentorship program is designed help you thrive by creating valuable learning experiences and meaningful developmental opportunities.

Goals Career Development: Connect early and mid-career professionals, as well as students, with established industry professionals to further career growth and talent development
Skill Enhancement: Build and create a sustainable community of support through collaboration, networking and skill building
Recruitment: Attract students to the tech industry by providing insights on the wide array of career opportunities
Executive Leadership Development: Promote executive leadership development opportunities that will encourage diversity within the ranks
